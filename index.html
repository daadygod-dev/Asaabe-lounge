Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Built for Today

Asaabe Café & Eatery was born from a simple belief: that great food and exceptional coffee have the power to bring people together. Our name, "Asaabe," reflects our commitment to craftsmanship — every bean is ethically sourced, every dish is prepared with intention.

From our signature pour-overs to our seasonal dinner menu, we celebrate local ingredients and global flavors. Whether you're joining us for a morning meeting, a leisurely brunch, or an intimate dinner, Asaabe is your sanctuary in the city.
